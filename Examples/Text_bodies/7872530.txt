Ye Ali, Tommy Genesis and Wes Period form baby.daddi

by Courtney Buck, 03 June 2016

You may remember these three artists coming together back in April for 'Young Girl', but now LA artists Ye Ali and Wes Period come together with Awful Records' Tommy Genesis to officially form baby.daddi. The eponymous track, produced by Chuck Inglish, ESTA and Genesis himself, offers a slight tropical adventure in its production while everyone delivers a little something different. This could be the start of something big.
