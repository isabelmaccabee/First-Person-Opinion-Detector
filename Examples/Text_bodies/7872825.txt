The Canadian Press

Published Friday, June 3, 2016 10:29AM EDT

LONGUEUIL, Que. -- A four-year-old boy is in stable condition after falling from a fourth-floor balcony on Montreal's south shore.

The incident occurred in Longueuil on Thursday night.

Police said today the toddler is in stable condition after suffering severe injuries and being taken to hospital.

Doctors no longer fear for his life.

Authorities are investigating the cause of the child's fall.
