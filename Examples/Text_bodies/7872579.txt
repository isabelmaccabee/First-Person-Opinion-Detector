Media accreditation for 2016 Youth Day commemoration

Members of the media are invited to apply for accreditation to cover the National Youth Day Commemoration taking place on 16 June 2016 at Orlando Stadium, Soweto.

The Youth Day will be commemorated under the theme: "Youth Moving South Africa Forward".

To apply for media accreditation, kindly complete online registration form on the link http://www.gcis.gov.za/content/media-registration

Deadline for submission of applications is Monday, 6 June 2016 at 12h00.

Issued by: Government Communications
